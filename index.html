About

An independent group focused on digital consumer brands.

Illyria Group is dedicated to building and managing e-commerce businesses through a model centered on ownership, execution, and long-term development. Rather than operating as a traditional agency or service provider, the group focuses on creating, structuring, and scaling its own digital assets.

Operating Model

Portfolio-based by design.

Illyria Group operates as a portfolio structure in which each brand is developed as an independent business asset. The group provides centralized expertise in positioning, acquisition, and operational execution while maintaining a long-term approach to value creation.
